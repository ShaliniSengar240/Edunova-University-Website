About Our University

UGC recognized with NAAC A++ accreditation.

Our Mission

EduNova University is a modern institution focused on shaping confident, skilled, and future-ready graduates. We combine academic excellence with innovation and practical learning to help students thrive in a fast-changing world.
We are dedicated to providing exceptional education that empowers students to become leaders and innovators in their chosen fields. Through rigorous academics, cutting-edge research, and a commitment to community service, we prepare our students to make meaningful contributions to society.

Our History

Founded in 1950, our university has grown from a small college with just 200 students to a prestigious institution serving over 15,000 students from around the globe. Throughout our history, we have remained committed to academic excellence, innovation, and service to our community.

Over the decades, we have expanded our programs, built state-of-the-art facilities, and attracted world-renowned faculty. Our alumni have gone on to become leaders in business, government, science, arts, and numerous other fields.
